The Public Works SF would like to invite you to join the ranks of the LOCAL 161!

LOCAL 161 is the community engagement division of The Public Works SF. As a hub for cultural activity in San Francisco, The Public Works SF event space welcomes the Bay Area community at large to participate in our mission to "Give the people what they want".

We believe that the people want good times, good music, and good company in safe, welcoming spaces. We believe this ethos extends beyong our dance floor, and requires diligence and action.

By joining LOCAL 161, you are pledging the following:

  • Participate in The Public Works SF mission to "Give the people what they want" - good times, good music, and good company.
  • Attend Public Works events as often as I am able.
  • Receive correspondence and communications from The Public Works SF which may include (but are not limited to) offering for free drinks, free entry to the space, free merchandise, and various other prizes and gifts.
  • Treat my fellow humans with respect & compassion.
  • Encourage the growth of art, creativity and community.
